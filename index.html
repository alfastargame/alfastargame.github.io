Four Images

A fun and challenging word game by AlfaStarGame

Can you guess the word just by looking at four pictures?
Four Images is a creative puzzle game that tests your logic and imagination. Look at the four seemingly unrelated images, think outside the box, and find the common word. The game is simple to start — but truly rewarding as you advance.
